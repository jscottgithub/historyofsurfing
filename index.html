A History of Surfing

Introduction

Surfing is a surface water sport in which the wave rider, referred to as a surfer, rides on the forward or deep face of a moving wave, which is usually carrying the surfer towards the shore. The history of surfing goes back hundreds of years to ancient Polynesian cultures, and it has become a modern-day cultural phenomenon.
